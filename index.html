But what's a Component?
It is the "master" version of an element.
Any changes you make here will
automatically apply to all its copies.

But remember, this only works one way:
If you change a copy, it won't affect
the master or other copies.

Feel free to modify your Component
Library as you need and watch your
whole design update automatically.
